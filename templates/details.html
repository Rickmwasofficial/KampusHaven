About KampusHaven

KampusHaven is your campus community's central hub for reconnecting students with their lost belongings. We understand that losing important items like your student ID, laptop, or personal belongings can be stressful and disruptive to your academic life. Our platform makes it simple to:

  • Post lost items you've found on campus
  • Search for items you've lost
  • Share items which are posted to Whatsapp
  • Receive Notification When an item is claimed

About

Created by students for students, KampusHaven aims to foster a helpful campus community where looking out for each other's belongings becomes second nature.
